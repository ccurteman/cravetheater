About Us

Crave is an immersive surrealist theatre company focused on making visually and emotionally moving contemporary theatre that encourages dialogue about pressing social, economic, and political issues. Founded by Sarah Marie Andrews and Kylie Jenifer Rose through our mutual love of transformative theatre, Crave Theatre Company is passionate about creating theatre for the now tailored to our community’s needs, and we use valuable audience feedback to determine each season’s thematic focus. This year’s MainStage production, "RED" by John Logan, focuses on the importance of empathy and finding a way to use your voice with intention to inspire change in the world around you. "RED" is based on Mark Rothko’s painting of his Seagram Murals with his (fictional) assistant Ken. This play shows a true craftsman making art with intention and purpose as the world pushes back. To us, what is most important about Rothko and Ken is how each character feels about their work as an artist and what they want their art to say. With that in mind, we want this play to be seen in a way that it hasn’t been seen before, and with the permission of the playwright, John Logan, we will be casting two women instead of two men because we feel that gender is not what matters in this play and that a female interpretation can reveal different layers to this text. Our all-female cast production of “RED” performs June 14th-30th, 2019 and will feature the acting talent of Maia McCarthy as Mark Rothko and Kylie Jenifer Rose as Ken. ​ We are also pleased to announce that as part of an Open Residency Program with Shaking the Tree Theatre we will be presenting a sitzprobe-style reading of a new devised work: a one woman musical featuring Kylie Jenifer Rose in March of 2019 entitled "Bad World."
